Final Year Project: Designing a Rocket for Autonomous Landing

My final year project is focused on designing, testing, and launching a multi-stage rocket that can take off with a payload and execute controlled autonomous landing.

The rocket design consists of:

- Two booster stages to maximise the altitude achieved by the rocket

- Version 1: A landing engine module with a directable nozzle system attached to a pressurized engine for controlled descent

- Version 2: Parachute-drone hybrid for precision landing without an additional engine

Our work involves real-world prototype testing, simulation modeling, and iterative design improvements to compare actual flight data with predictive models.
