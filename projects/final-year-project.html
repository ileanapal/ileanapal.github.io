Final Year Project: Designing a Rocket for Autonomous Landing

My **final year project** is focused on **designing, testing, and launching a multi-stage rocket** that can **take off with a payload and execute controlled autonomous landing**.

The **rocket design** consists of: - **Two booster stages** for maximum altitude - **A landing engine module** for controlled descent - **Parachute-drogue hybrid & directable nozzle systems** for precision landing Our work involves **real-world testing**, **simulation modeling**, and **iterative design improvements** to compare actual flight data with predictive models.
