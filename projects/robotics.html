Perceptual Robotics Competition

The Perceptual Robotics Competition challenged teams to design and program a robot that could detect and shoot a laser at dynamic targets using monocular vision. Our team competed through three progressive stages, making it to the final three teams in the last round.

Our robot was built with: - Computer vision algorithms for detecting and tracking moving targets. - PID control systems for precise laser aiming and improved accuracy and hit rate. The system was tested under real-world conditions, and we achieved high-speed, accurate targeting using real-time computer vision feedback.
