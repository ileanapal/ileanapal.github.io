Liquid Propellant Rocket with Controlled Landing

For my final year project, I designed and built a liquid-propellant rocket capable of controlled landing using deployable parachutes and advanced control mechanisms. The project involved working with high-pressure propellants, optimizing the combustion chamber design, and designing the nozzle.

The landing system used a custom-built deployment mechanism, and my simulations were performed using OpenRocket and MATLAB for trajectory prediction.

Technical Details:

  • Propulsion System: Liquid bipropellant engine
  • Control System: Parachute deployment with microcontroller
  • Max Altitude: ~500 meters
